About Bestie Bakes

Our Story

Bestie Bakes was founded in 2018 by best friends Fortunate Dlamini and Pretty Maleka. What started as a small home-baking blog quickly grew into a community-loved artisan bakery. Fortunate, a classically trained pastry chef, and Pretty, a business strategist, combined their talents to create a space where quality ingredients and warm hospitality come together.

Today, Bestie Bakes has two thriving locations and a loyal following of customers who appreciate the love baked into every cake and pastry.

Our Mission

Mission: To create joy through exceptional baked goods made with integrity, using locally-sourced, sustainable ingredients, while fostering a sense of community around every table.

Vision: To become the most loved neighborhood bakery brand, inspiring connection through the art of baking.
